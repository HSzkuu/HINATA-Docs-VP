## Connection

Use a data cable to connect your device to the HINATA card reader so HINATA Go can access it.

## Usage

After connecting, tap this bar at the bottom of the screen to enter the details page:

![hinata-device](go/features/assets/hinata-device.jpg)

The details page is shown below. You can configure or update the HINATA device here.
